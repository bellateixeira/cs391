About Me

From a Professional Perspective

A detail-oriented and globally experienced Business Analyst with a passion for analyzing complex business processes, identifying opportunities for improvement, and delivering strategic solutions to drive organizational success. SAFe 6.0 Certified in agile methodologies, with expertise in project management, technical architecture design, and machine learning applications. Proficient in Python, SQL, and Java, with a strong ability to manage projects from inception to completion, lead cross-functional teams, and rapidly acquire technical skills.

Rooted in a multicultural background spanning South Africa, Portugal, and the United States, I bring a unique global perspective that fosters adaptability, empathy, and innovation. My extensive travels have honed my ability to navigate diverse cultural environments and contribute to inclusive, data-driven decision-making. Experienced in developing and executing machine learning programs, with a solid understanding of artificial intelligence education, and adept at parsing databases to extract critical insights.

Recognized for exceptional communication, empathy, and interpersonal skills, I thrive at the intersection of technology, data, and human collaboration. A quick learner with a proven track record of delivering innovative solutions in dynamic and complex settings, I am dedicated to fostering a collaborative and positive work environment while driving strategic goals.

From a Personal Perspective

I am the proud dog mom of a one-year-old Bernese Mountain Dog, Poppy (or Popperino, Popperoni, Popster, Chicken Nuggest, Sweet Angel, Popsalot...depending on the day)! I love spending my free time snuggling her on the couch (she is a big snuggle bug) and watching a great show with my loved ones. I enjoy staying active and waking up early to keep up with my consistent morning routine. I am on a mission to limit my screen time (given the nature of my work) so I love winding down with a good book, a warm drink and my comfiest pajamas. I am learning an old skill - acoustic guitar - as I used to play in high school and enjoy the challenge beyond simply a deep love for music instilled in me from my dad. I am a traveler by heart but a creature of routine. There is so much more I could share but for now I will leave you with my favorite poem (courtesy of my dad):

Out of the night that covers me,

Black as the pit from pole to pole,

I thank whatever gods may be

For my unconquerable soul.

In the fell clutch of circumstance

I have not winced nor cried aloud.

Under the bludgeonings of chance

My head is bloody, but unbowed.

Beyond this place of wrath and tears

Looms but the Horror of the shade,

And yet the menace of the years

Finds and shall find me unafraid.

It matters not how strait the gate,

How charged with punishments the scroll,

I am the master of my fate,

I am the captain of my soul.
